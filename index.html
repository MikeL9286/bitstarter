Don't you hate having to find spots to meet up with friends? Wouldn't it be nice to have an app that can find places between the two of you?

Well lucky for you, Meet-Me will exist to do just that!

FAQ

How does Meet-Me work?

Meet-Me uses your current address + a friend's address to find a mid-point between you and your friend. It then searches around that location for points of interest.

You can then get directions to that spot and send the address to your friend so they can do the same.

Why should I tweet?
